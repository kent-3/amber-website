About

Amber DAO is a community-led DeFi experiment in sound money.

AMBER is the token governing the Amber DAO.

The goal of the Amber DAO is to make AMBER the go-to store-of-value token in the Secret Network ecosystem.

The total supply of AMBER is 8888.

Airdrop Eligibility Criteria

$SCRT Stakers

$SCRT delegators staking a minimum of 44.44 $SCRT with an eligible validator by block 4008888 will receive an airdrop of 0.2 $AMBER
